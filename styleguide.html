Usage Notes
Primary Font
Barrio — all display headings, logo, and section titles. Never used for body copy.
Secondary Font
Inter — all body text, labels, buttons, navigation, and UI elements.
Dark Sections
Background: #0a5284. Text: white or #94D2FF. Used for hero, header, tide tracker.
Light Sections
Background: #f2efea (cream). Text: #1a1a1a. Used for content cards and about sections.
Gold Accent
#FFBA0D — exceptional tide ratings, highlight callouts. Use sparingly.
Rust
#853700 — subtle labels and secondary text on cream backgrounds only.
